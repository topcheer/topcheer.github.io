‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What should I do if AI features are not working?

A: Please ensure you have correctly configured your API key in Settings. You need a Zhipu AI or Google AI Studio account with a valid API key.

Q: How do I save my artwork?

A: Tap the "Save to Gallery" button in the AI generation results screen to save your artwork to your device's photo gallery.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: Currently we support Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Russian, Portuguese, Italian, Turkish, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Arabic, and Hindi.

Q: How do I switch AI providers?

A: In the Settings page, you can choose to use either Zhipu AI or Google AI Studio as your AI service provider.

üîß Troubleshooting

Network Connection Issues

Please ensure your device is connected to the internet and the connection is stable. AI features require an internet connection to work properly.

Permission Issues

If you cannot save artwork to your gallery, please check if the app has been granted storage permissions. You can manually authorize permissions in your device settings.

Performance Optimization Tips

  • Close unnecessary background apps for better performance
  • Ensure sufficient storage space on your device
  • Regularly clear app cache
  • Update the app to the latest version
